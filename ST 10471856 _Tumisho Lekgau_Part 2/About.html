About:

T-Exotics Founded in 2012 by Tumisho Lekgau, T-Exotics is a private dealership specializing in rare and exotic vehicles—

from vintage classics to the latest high-performance models. Initially struggling with visibility,

the dealership gained traction in 2014 and now targets petrolheads and car enthusiasts with a passion for unique automobiles. The company’s mission is to sell rare cars across South Africa and eventually expand internationally.
